# Google Form Setup Guide for NC-SDESS 2026 Registration

## Registration Form Fields (from Excel Sheet)

### Basic Information
- **Name** (Short text)
- **Email** (Email)
- **Phone Number** (Short text)
- **Organization/College** (Short text)
- **Department** (Dropdown/Multiple choice)

### Participation Details
- **Category** (Multiple choice):
  - UG Student
  - PG Student
  - Research Scholar
  - Faculty
  - Industry Professional
  
- **Participation Mode** (Multiple choice):
  - Presenter (Paper/Abstract)
  - Poster Presenter
  - Attendee Only

- **Track** (Dropdown - Select one of 7 tracks):
  1. Sustainable Energy Solutions & Clean Power Technologies
  2. Smart Electronics & Sensor-Based Solutions
  3. Software Systems & Cyber Solutions
  4. AI & Data-Driven Solutions
  5. Cyber-Physical Systems & Intelligent Automation
  6. Sustainable Materials, Applied Physics & Engineering Innovations
  7. Integrated Engineering Solutions for Sustainable Society

### Professional Information
- **Organization Type** (For non-student):
  - Academic Institution
  - Industry/Private
  - Research Laboratory
  - Government Organization
  - Other

- **Membership** (Checkboxes - Select all that apply):
  - ISAMPE
  - ISNT
  - IEOM
  - IEEE
  - IUCEE-EWB
  - IETE
  - ISTE
  - CSI
  - None

### Submission Details (For Presenters)
- **Submission Type** (If Presenter selected):
  - Paper Presentation
  - Abstract/Poster
  
- **Paper Title** (Conditional - if submitting)
- **Abstract/Description** (Long text)

### Attendance Mode
- **Attendance Mode** (Multiple choice):
  - Offline (In-person at HITAM)
  - Online (Virtual)

### Fees & Registration
- **Early Bird Registration**: Available until January 15, 2026
  - Student (UG/PG): ₹400
  - Research Scholar: ₹600
  - Faculty: ₹800
  - Industry: ₹1200

- **Regular Registration**: January 16-25, 2026
  - Student (UG/PG): ₹500
  - Research Scholar: ₹800
  - Faculty: ₹1000
  - Industry: ₹1500

- **Registration Deadline**: January 25, 2026

### Additional Information
- **Dietary Preferences** (For catering - if applicable)
- **Special Requirements** (Long text - accessibility, etc.)
- **Agree to Terms** (Checkbox - required)

## How to Create the Google Form

1. Go to **forms.google.com**
2. Create a new form with title: "NC-SDESS 2026 - Registration"
3. Add the fields listed above in order
4. Use conditional logic to show/hide fields based on participation mode
5. Set required fields: Name, Email, Phone, Organization, Category, Track, Attendance Mode
6. Customize the form colors to match brand (Green: #2e7d32)
7. Set response destination to collect in Google Sheets

---

## Form URL Format
Once created, the form will be available at:

https://forms.gle/[FORM_ID]
